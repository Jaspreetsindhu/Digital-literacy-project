Digital Literacy Project Report - CSE0001

Digital Literacy Introduction (100-150 words)

In today's fast changing digital world, being digitally literate is important for student success academically and professionally. I undertook this project as part of the CSE0001 Digital Literacy course where I acted as a Student Digital Ambassador. This project consists of creating an electronic portfolio that illustrates a student's knowledge, practical skills and appropriate use of digital technologies.

There are five objectives of the project, each corresponding to an area of digital literacy. These include increasing awareness of digital literacy, maintaining a professional online presence, using digital technology platforms, communicating respectfully and safely using technology and protecting oneself from cybercrime. Through this project, I have gained practical experience with many digital tools and understand more about responsible and safe digital behaviour.
