# Prevention checklist:


Cyber Safety Tips for Students

* Always check if links, emails and messages are real before clicking on them.
* Don't share one-time passwords, passwords or bank details with anyone even if they say they are from a trusted organization.
* Use different passwords for different accounts and change them often.
* Try to use two-factor authentication wherever possible for security.
* Don't use Wi-Fi for things like online banking or sensitive activities.
* Keep your devices updated with the security fixes and antivirus software.
* Be careful of messages that try to scare you or make you feel urgent, like threats of account suspension.
* Check your bank statements and transaction history regularly for any activity.

​UPI & Financial Safety Tips
* Never agree to UPI payment requests or "collect requests.”
* Check the receiver’s name and UPI ID again before sending money.
* Don't scan QR codes from people you don't know especially if they sent them to you.

Reporting Cybercrime
* Report incidents at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https\://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 ( 24×7, in India)


# 🛡️ Cyber Safety Checklist for Students

## 🔐 General Cyber Safety

- [ ] Always verify links, emails, and messages are legitimate before clicking on them.
- [ ] Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone — even those claiming to be from a trusted organization.
- [ ] Use a different password for each account and update them regularly.
- [ ] Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- [ ] Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking or any sensitive activities.
- [ ] Keep all devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
- [ ] Be cautious of messages that create urgency or fear (e.g., threats of account suspension).
- [ ] Regularly review your bank statements and transaction history for any suspicious activity.

## 💳 UPI & Financial Safety

- [ ] Never approve UPI payment requests or "collect requests" you did not initiate.
- [ ] Always double-check the receiver's name and UPI ID before sending money.
- [ ] Do not scan QR codes received from unknown sources or unsolicited senders.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime

- [ ] Report incidents at the **National Cyber Crime Portal**: [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
- [ ] Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline**: `1930` *(available 24×7 in India)*
