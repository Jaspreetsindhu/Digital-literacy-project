# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## Things You Should Do

- [ ] Always use a professional and respectful tone in posts and comments.
- [ ] Protect your privacy by updating the security settings on your accounts.
- [ ] Share only information you know to be accurate and true.
- [ ] Think carefully before posting — make sure it represents you in a positive light.
- [ ] Use Social Media to network, learn new things, and develop skills.
- [ ] Respect differing opinions and engage in constructive conversations.
- [ ] Keep your profiles clean and appropriate for anyone to view (e.g., teachers, employers).

## Things You Should Not Do

- [ ] Do not post personal information such as your phone number, address, or passwords.
- [ ] Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
- [ ] Do not share news or information you haven't verified to be true.
- [ ] Do not engage in arguments, harassment, or bullying of any kind.
- [ ] Do not click suspicious links or respond to messages from unknown senders.
- [ ] Avoid oversharing personal details about your private life.
- [ ] Do not post anything that could damage your reputation or future opportunities.
